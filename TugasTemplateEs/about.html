    Es Lilin

    Ice lolly initially only has a very simple taste. In the early 1950s, many residents in the rural areas of Bandung were selling this cold and refreshing light snack.

    At first they made ice wax with one taste, which is a combination of coconut milk, ice flour and frozen sugar, the ice-es are then sold around the city of Bandung. Since then the ice lolly has become synonymous with the city nicknamed the flower

    Besides coconut milk and sugar, at the beginning of its appearance, ice wax was also popular with two distinctive flavors, namely green beans and young coconut.
